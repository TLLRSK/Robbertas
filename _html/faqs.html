FAQS

Who are you?
We are those who dwell in the shadows, guardians of silence beauty. We started with a simple purpose, but with a profound impact: to create art through fashion and bring it to the right people.
What do you do?
Each season, we join hands with talented designers and artists to create limited collections of balaclavas, each with its own meaning and personality.
Where are you?
We are nestled in the heart of Italy, in a secluded workshop surrounded by the rolling hills of the countryside.

Only the hushed whispers of our designers can be heard as they carefully plan each stitch and seam with precision.
